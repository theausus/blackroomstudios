Basic - Complete Song Production

Blackroom Studios offers several different levels of complete song production, and all include Recording, Editing, Mixing, Mastering, and Production. Basically, this is when your band books a session - then comes to the studio to track all of the instruments - I will be editing the music to be tight and cohesive throughout the day - post-production will then consist of any remaining edits, mixing, and finally mastering - I will then send the music to you through "Dropbox". My continuous experience with bands and music allows me to have knowledgeable opinions and contributions to your music as an outsider or non-band member. Just let me know the level of producer involvement that you desire.

Basic Recording Session + Editing/Mixing/Mastering - Rates vary per song.

This is the basic Blackroom Studios session for the typical full band recording, which consists of tracking, editing, mixing, and mastering. Time correction of drums, bass, guitars, vocals, etc. is common for the "Basic Recording Session," as well as pitch correction and other editing techniques. Due to major fluctuation between different genres, length of songs, number/arrangement of instruments, etc., flat rates will vary. Please message with detailed information for estimates.


Low Production/Hourly Session + Quick Edit/Mix/Master - $40 per hour

The Low Production route is directed towards bands or individual artists that need less recording time for smaller projects. All editing, mixing, or mastering desired will be done during the session.


Mixing/Mastering Session - $40 per hour

This option is directed towards bands that would like to book a short session to return to the studio to make final mixing/mastering adjustments (as a group) to a set of songs (or full CD) that have recently been recorded at Blackroom. Sometimes this is a good idea for bands who may want to request many changes or tweaks to the final mixes that they receive. To obtain the desired sound, it may be easier to verbalize, discuss, and hear these adjustments in the studio as a whole.


The listed sessions and prices are a common layout that may be applicable for the majority of bands and independent artists. Although, circumstances and prices may vary depending on the needs of the musical group. If your desired project does not seem to match up with the listed session options, please feel free to contact with more information to determine the best route for you and your band.
