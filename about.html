Luke Garrigus
- Audio Engineer/Producer/Musician

I began my musical career at the age of twelve. While taking guitar lessons from John Defoore at the Beckham Hotel ( just down the street from the future Blackroom Studios ), I was also learning to write and record music. I continued on to write and perform with numerous bands of rock, pop, acoustic, indie, and metal genres. I was developing a taste and an ear for as many musical styles as possible. Blackroom Studios was later established in 2010. I had fallen in love with the creation of music and the endless capabilities that audio recording had to offer. I am currently a music major at the University of Texas at Tyler with a focus in classical guitar. As my knowledge of music has increased, so have my skills as a producer and audio engineer. Now, I strive to capture musical sound to the best of my abilities. I am constantly learning and continuously improving. I work extremely hard on every project that comes my way. I would love to work with you. Let's make some music.

Blackroom Studios

For an interesting historical tip, the studio building originated as a doctor's clinic during the 1950's. It has now been set up as a professional home recording studio. As I am a full time college student, the studio is generally operated for weekend sessions, except during the summer when booking is open for weekdays as well (often weekday session pricing may be discounted, contact for details). Blackroom Studios provides a relaxed, yet professional environment enabling (you) artists to comfortably capture the desired sound. But overall, Blackroom offers great sound quality and hard work for low/affordable rates (refer to services/rates for pricing details). All genres are welcome. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
- Luke Garrigus
